Cut ¾ of the peeled
beetroot
into small dice (you may want to wear rubber gloves to do this) and set the rest aside. Melt the butter in a large pan, and then soften the onion over a gentle heat for 5 minutes. Add the
carrot,
leek,
celery,
diced beetroot,
allspice and bay leaf and stir well to coat with butter. Cook for another 10 minutes, adding a little stock if the vegetables begin to look dry. Meanwhile, grate the remaining
beetroot
. Pour in the rest of the stock and the
potatoes
and simmer for 15 minutes, then add the
cabbage
, garlic
and grated
beetroot
. Cook until all the vegetables are tender (about 10 minutes). Add the vinegar, sugar, pepper and a pinch of salt and taste. Add a little more of any of these if necessary, then serve with a dollop of sour cream, a sprig of
dill
, and some Polish bread on the side.
